My Interests

Programming

As I mentioned before, programming is one of my favorite hobbies. Video games are my favorite thing to mess around with when it comes to programming. I actually began to teach myself Java so that I could make my own games which is how I started coding. The process of starting from scratch on a project to seeing the final product is always the best feeling ever. This is why I've also wanted to develop software professionally.

Sewing

One of my other favorite hobbies is sewing. I like sewing for the same reason I like programming, starting from scratch and ending up with a completely custom product. One advantage with sewing is that you have a little for freedom to make mistakes without them being too detrimental to your project. This makes it less of a problem solving activity and a bit more relaxing which is nice. This is a picture of the first reupholstery project I ever did. However, I started sewing to make my own shirts.

Skating

I started skating around the same time that I started programming but after a while I stopped for a few years. Just recently however, I got back into it. Whenever I feel like i've been stuck inside for too long, it's nice to go outside and skate for a while. The picture here is of the first time I did a back-side boardslide. This was a huge accomplishment for me and made me feel like I was getting a lot better.
